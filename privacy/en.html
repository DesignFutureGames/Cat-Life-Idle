2. Information Collected and Processed

The Developer does not operate a proprietary account registration system or membership database, and the Service is not designed on the assumption that users’ personal information will be continuously stored on the Developer’s own servers. However, the following information may be processed while using the Service.

  • Information stored locally on the device: Game progress, settings, temporary data, and other data stored on the user’s device
  • When using Google Play Games sign-in: Player identification information, sign-in status, achievements, leaderboard records, and information related to gameplay activity
  • When using Google Play Billing: Purchase status, transaction status, and transaction-related information required to verify or restore purchases
  • When using Google AdMob: Device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP addresses, user interaction information, diagnostic information, and other information required for advertising, ad measurement, and fraud prevention
  • When using Firebase Analytics: App usage information, event records, usage patterns, device and app environment information, and identifiers used for analytics
  • When using Firebase Crashlytics: App crash information, error logs, device status at the time of a crash, operating system information, app version, and diagnostic information required to analyze app stability

Much of the information described above may be processed automatically through external services and SDKs such as Google Play Games, Google Play Billing, Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics, and Firebase Crashlytics, rather than being entered directly by users or collected directly by the Developer.

3. Purposes of Processing Information

The Developer may process information within the scope necessary to provide and operate the Service for the following purposes.

  • Providing basic functions such as running the game, saving settings, and maintaining game progress
  • Providing Google Play Games sign-in, achievement, and leaderboard features
  • Processing in-app purchases and verifying purchase restoration through Google Play Billing
  • Displaying advertisements, measuring advertising performance, and preventing advertising fraud
  • Analyzing usage patterns, improving content and features, and optimizing Service operations
  • Analyzing and responding to app crashes, errors, abnormal termination, and performance issues
  • Detecting and preventing fraud, misuse, abuse, and abnormal activity

4. Retention Period

Because the Developer does not operate a membership database on its own servers, the Developer generally does not separately retain personal information for an extended period.

  • Data stored locally on the device: Such data may be deleted when the app is removed, app data is reset, the device is changed, or the user manually deletes the data.
  • Information related to Google Play Games, Google Play Billing, Google AdMob, and Firebase: Retention periods may vary depending on each service provider’s policies, technical structure, account status, and applicable laws.
  • Support emails: Emails may be retained for a reasonable period to respond to user inquiries and resolve disputes.
